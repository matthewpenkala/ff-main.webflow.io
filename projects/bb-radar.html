What were the goals of these product demos?

This study is simply one example: a revamped product launch. We live in an era of digital marketing with limited attention spans. Pitch decks & specification sheets may be educational, but you must ask yourself: "How do we get them in the door?" Of course, this is accomplished by captivating the customer in an entertaining yet still informative way. As with most things, there is a delicate balance here that must be kept in mind. Such was precisely what we set out to do with BlackBerry® Radar™. In each video, you'll notice there are bits of technical information sprinkled throughout, but also mixed with solutions to common customer difficulties. The scriptwriting & voice over, paired with custom-made motion graphics, is what ties everything together into a truly professional piece of content.
